WHAT ARE YOUR THOUGHTS ON ENGAGEMENT SESSIONS?

Engagement sessions are my very favourite for numerous reasons. Most importantly, they let you get acquainted with me and being in front of the camera so come your big day - you feel much more relaxed. I also get a feel of how you interact with one another, which in turn makes me more comfortable with my clients too. Plus, you get awesome photos with a more easy going vibe in your regular attire that you get to show case. The fun we have is just a bonus!
DO YOU OFFER PRODUCTS SUCH AS ALBUMS?

I sure do and I am all for them! Albums and prints allow you to showcase your images in a more tangible way. In our digital age, it pains me to think of all the beautiful work collecting dust on a computer. What better way to decorate your home than with a gorgeous wood print or canvas over your mantle?!
